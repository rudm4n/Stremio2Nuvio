Login to Stremio

Enter your Stremio credentials to fetch your installed addons. Your data is never sent to our servers — all API calls go directly to api.strem.io.

How to get your AuthKey

  1. Open web.stremio.com and sign in
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools → Console
  3. Paste: JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('profile')).auth.key
  4. Copy the output and paste it below

Select Addons to Transfer

Choose which addons you want to transfer to Nuvio. Each addon will be opened for installation one by one.

Transfer to Nuvio

Each addon will open in Nuvio for installation. Make sure Nuvio is installed on your device.

Click "Install Next Addon" to start. Nuvio will open to install each addon — confirm the installation in the app, then come back to continue.
Tip: If Nuvio doesn't open automatically, you can also copy the manifest URL and paste it manually in Nuvio under Addons → Add by URL.
